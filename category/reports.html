Guides and examples of Immersve reports.

📄️ Introduction

Immersve can generate reports for a given card program over a time period that suits your needs.

📄️ Authorization Reports

The authorization report can be used to review all card purchase authorizations for a given period.

📄️ Clearing Reports

The clearing report can be used to review all card purchase clearings for a given period.
